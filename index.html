Hi there!

I'm a graduate assistant at the Human-Centered Artificial Intelligence Research Laboratory at the University of Central Florida, a new sister lab to the Complex Adaptive Systems Laboratory.

I'm interested in using computational means to help answer big questions about human societies, organizations, teams, and cultures. With a background in space engineering, I'm also particularly interested in understanding the social structures and practices that will best sustain human settlements in space and on other worlds.

I believe that by developing and leveraging advances in computing, we can better understand the behavior of human beings. Through this understanding, we can transform our society into a more sustainable, equitable, and compassionate place for everyone.
