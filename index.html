Hi, I'm Lux (like the SI unit for lumens per square metre!

I'm a PIBBSS fellow and a PhD student at the Uppsala Social Robotics Laboratory in Sweden. My current work is focused on the ethics of robot identity construction and feminist robot design.

Ethical AI that is aligned to human values has tremendous potential to transform our society into a more sustainable, equitable, and compassionate place for everyone. My mission, in a broad sense, is to see through this transformation.

My research interests to this end are broad and range from work in fundamental theoretics to immediate real-world applications:

  • My master's thesis was on getting AI to understand the social factors that foster human cooperation.
  • I've analyzed the evolution of entire cultures over millennia to get a better grip on how these "society" things of ours grow and change in the long-run.
  • A project I helped lead went to space (though that one was just for fun).
  • I helped create (and currently steward) the world's most complete dataset of archaeological radiocarbon dates so that we can better understand what did and didn't work for our ancestors.
